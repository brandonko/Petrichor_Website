A Closer Look

What is aromatherapy? Aromatherapy is a healing treatment that uses natural plant extracts to promote health and wellbeing. It uses aromatic essential oils medicinally to improve the health of the body, mind, and spirit. Our bracelets are equipped with porous lava beads so that you can wear the bracelet, drip the essential oils onto the lava beads, and let your body begin healing.

Our Process

1

Stringing the Bracelet

Each bracelet is strung bead by bead and secured with multiple knots at the end.

2

Securing the Knot

We use industrial strength glue over the knot to make sure the bracelet stays together.

3

Fastening the Cover

Sterling silver crimp beads are then attached onto the bracelet over the knot.

4

Ready for You

Each bracelet is carefully crafted with precision, tailoring to customers with various wrist sizes.

We use gemstones and lava beads strung on quality elastic. The bracelet is held together with industry duty glue. Each bracelet is handmade curated by one of our team members. We then fill our dropper applicator with our specialized essential oil mixture; two parts filtered water, one part essential oil. Each oil holding individual healing properties and significance to you. We finish your bracelet off by sealing it in a reusable cloth bag. Keeping eco-friendly options at the forefront of our production, we are mindful of the waste from each ingredient. And rest is history!

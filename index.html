The Values We Wear

Find Your Values

We are here to help you discover, design, and display your values. We want you to discover what values you hold close to your heart, so you can continue to wear them on your wrist. Each gemstone is connected to a core belief and accessorize your style and character

Well Being

We believe in the importance of self-care and the prioritization of mental health. Aromatherapy is here to help and we want to bring its healing properties to you, specifically around your wrist. Destress, boost your immune system, clear your head, all possibilities with Petrichor

Sustainablity

We believe in standing up for our home, Earth. In all our decisions, we work to reduce our waste and include Mother Nature in the conversation. We are constantly doing our best to wear our values with every carefully sourced gemstone and reusable cloth packaging

Our Process

We listen to you. We take your requests for size, gems, layout, and scents. With each personalized order, we listen to all your requests and turn your ideas into reality

We use gemstones and lava beads strung on quality elastic. The bracelet is held together with industry duty glue. Each bracelet is handmade curated by one of our team members

We then fill our rollerball applicator with our specialized essential oil mixture; two parts filtered water, one part essential oil. Each oil holding individual healing properties and significance to you. + UV proof amber glass containers

We finish your bracelet off by sealing it in a reusable cloth bag. Keeping eco-friendly options at the forefront of our production, we are mindful of the waste from each ingredient

And rest is history!
